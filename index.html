FAQ
GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does a campaign take to go live?+
Once we receive your brief and confirm the campaign, creators can start posting within 24 hours. Most campaigns see significant view volume within the first 3–5 days.
How do you track views?+
Our team manually verifies view counts across all submitted links on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Creators submit through our Discord bot, and we track performance in real time.
What's the minimum budget to start?+
Campaign pricing varies based on your goals, platform, and content type. Every campaign is custom-quoted — reach out via Schedule a Call and we'll work out the right scope for you.
What platforms do you work on?+
We support TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, X (Twitter), and Facebook Reels — 5 platforms total.
How do I join as a clipper?+
Clippers join exclusively through our Discord server at discord.gg/akgUVZRAwb. Read the rules, start picking up active campaigns, and open a ticket to receive your payout once you've generated views.
What types of content work best for campaigns?+
Short-form clips, reaction content, highlight edits, and UGC reviews consistently outperform static content. Our creators know what formats each platform's algorithm rewards.
