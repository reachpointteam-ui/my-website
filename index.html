FAQ
GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does a campaign take to go live?+
Once we receive your brief and confirm the campaign, creators can start posting within 24 hours. Most campaigns see significant view volume within the first 3–5 days.
How do you track views?+
Our team manually verifies view counts across all submitted links on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Creators submit their links through our Discord bot, and we track performance in real time.
What's the minimum budget to start?+
Campaign pricing varies based on your goals, platform, and content type. Every campaign is custom-quoted — reach out via the Schedule a Call form and we'll work out the right scope for you.
Can I see proof of past campaigns?+
Yes. Join our Discord server and open a ticket on the main server — our team will share verified creator stats, past campaign results, and audience breakdowns for your category.
What platforms do you work on?+
We support TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, X (Twitter), and Facebook Reels — 5 platforms total. Creators can post on one or multiple platforms depending on your campaign brief.
How do I join as a clipper?+
Clippers join exclusively through our Discord server. Head to discord.gg/akgUVZRAwb, read the rules, and start picking up active campaigns. Once you've generated views, open a ticket on our server to receive your payment proof and payout.
